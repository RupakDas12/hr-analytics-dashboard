Key Findings


Overall attrition rate: 16.12 percent across 1,470 employees
Active headcount: 1,233, average employee age: 37
Department-wise attrition: Sales 56.12 percent, R&D 38.82 percent, HR 5.06 percent
Highest attrition concentration in the 25-34 age group (112 cases)
Job satisfaction varies by role, with Sales Executives and Research Scientists showing the highest survey response volume


Dashboard Components

1. KPI Summary Cards

Attrition rate, active headcount, and average age displayed as quick-glance metrics.

2. Department-wise Attrition

Pie chart showing Sales as the highest-attrition department, followed by R&D and HR.

3. Number of Employees by Age Group

Bar chart distribution of employee count across age ranges.

4. Job Satisfaction Rating

Heatmap-style table cross-referencing job roles against satisfaction scores (1-4 scale).

5. Education Field wise Attrition

Bar chart ranking attrition by educational background.

6. Attrition Rate by Gender for Different Age Groups

Donut charts segmenting attrition by gender across age brackets, with an Education filter for drill-down.
